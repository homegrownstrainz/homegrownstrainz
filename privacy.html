Privacy Policy

HomeGrownStrainz respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and disclose information when you use our website or purchase products from us.

1. Information We Collect

When you place an order with HomeGrownStrainz, we may collect personal information including:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Shipping address
  • Billing address

We collect this information only as necessary to process and fulfill your order and provide related customer services.

2. Payment Information

HomeGrownStrainz accepts payment exclusively through PayPal.

We do not store, retain, or have access to your payment card details, banking information, or other payment credentials. All payment transactions are processed securely through PayPal and are subject to PayPal’s own privacy and security policies.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use personal information to:

  • Process and fulfill orders.
  • Communicate with you regarding your order.
  • Provide customer support.
  • Comply with legal, regulatory, tax, and accounting obligations.

4. Address Information

Shipping and billing addresses are collected solely for order processing, fulfillment, delivery, and related customer service purposes.

Address information is retained only for as long as reasonably necessary to complete delivery of your order and satisfy applicable legal, accounting, taxation, or record-keeping obligations.

When such information is no longer required, it is securely deleted, destroyed, or anonymized.

5. Information Sharing

HomeGrownStrainz may share limited personal information with trusted third-party service providers where necessary to operate our business and fulfill orders.

This may include:

  • Shipping and postal carriers.
  • Payment processors such as PayPal.
  • Professional advisers where required by law.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

6. Data Security

HomeGrownStrainz implements reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, misuse, or destruction.

However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location and applicable privacy laws, you may have rights relating to your personal information, including:

  • Access to information we hold about you.
  • Correction of inaccurate information.
  • Request for deletion of personal information where legally permitted.

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the details below.

8. Changes to this Privacy Policy

HomeGrownStrainz reserves the right to modify this Privacy Policy at any time. Any updates will be posted on this page and will become effective immediately upon publication.
